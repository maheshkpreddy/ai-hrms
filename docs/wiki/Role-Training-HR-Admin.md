## 📋 Role Overview

The HR Admin role manages day-to-day HR operations including employee management, talent acquisition, attendance tracking, payroll processing, and performance management. HR Admins have broad access to HR-related modules but cannot modify system-level configurations or manage company settings. They focus on employee lifecycle management, recruitment, compliance, and workforce planning. This role is ideal for HR managers and HR business partners who need comprehensive HR functionality.

## 🔄 Key Workflows

The following are the primary workflows you will perform as a **HR Admin**:

1. Managing the complete employee lifecycle from onboarding to exit
2. Processing recruitment through the AI Talent Acquisition module
3. Running monthly payroll and managing expense claims
4. Conducting and overseeing performance review cycles
5. Managing attendance, shifts, and leave approvals
6. Monitoring HR analytics and generating compliance reports
7. Coordinating learning and development programs
