## 📋 Role Overview

The Recruiter role focuses on talent acquisition and recruitment operations. Recruiters have access to the AI Talent Acquisition module for managing job postings, candidate pipelines, interview scheduling, and offer management. They also have basic dashboard and self-service access. This role is designed for recruitment specialists and talent acquisition teams who need to manage the end-to-end hiring process efficiently using AI-powered tools.

## 🔄 Key Workflows

The following are the primary workflows you will perform as a **Recruiter**:

1. Creating and publishing job postings with AI assistance
2. Managing the candidate pool and reviewing applications
3. Scheduling and coordinating interviews with candidates and panelists
4. Generating and tracking offer letters
5. Managing the AI-powered onboarding process for new hires
6. Tracking recruitment metrics and time-to-hire analytics
