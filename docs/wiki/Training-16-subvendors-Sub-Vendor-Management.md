## 📋 Module Overview

The Sub Vendor Management module handles the complete lifecycle of staffing sub-vendor relationships. It enables organizations to manage vendor profiles, collect and track resumes from vendors, and monitor the candidate pipeline from submission through placement. The module provides visibility into vendor performance, resume quality, and placement rates, helping organizations optimize their vendor partnerships and streamline the staffing supply chain.

The Sub Vendor Management module is accessible from both the sidebar navigation and the module home page grid. It contains the following sub-items that provide specialized functionality within the module.

---

## 🔧 Sub-Items & Features

### 1. Vendors

Manage sub-vendor profiles with company details, contact information, specialization areas, and active status. Track resume count, placement rates, and vendor performance metrics.

**Steps to use Vendors:**
1. Navigate to the Sub Vendor Management module from the sidebar or module home page
2. Click on "Vendors" from the sub-menu
3. Use the available options to create, view, edit, or manage records

## 💡 Tips & Best Practices

1. Evaluate vendor performance regularly based on resume quality and placement conversion rates.
2. Maintain clear communication with vendors about job requirements and candidate expectations.
3. Track the entire pipeline to identify bottlenecks in the staffing process.
4. Use vendor specialization tags to match vendors with appropriate job openings.
5. Regularly update vendor status to reflect current partnerships accurately.
