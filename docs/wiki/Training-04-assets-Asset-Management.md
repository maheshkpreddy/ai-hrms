## 📋 Module Overview

The Asset Management module enables organizations to track and manage company assets throughout their lifecycle. From laptops and phones to access cards and equipment, the module provides complete visibility into asset inventory, assignment status, and condition tracking. Administrators can assign assets to employees, track asset requests, and manage asset returns during employee exits. The module supports various asset types and maintains a full audit trail of asset movements.

The Asset Management module is accessible from both the sidebar navigation and the module home page grid. It contains the following sub-items that provide specialized functionality within the module.

---

## 🔧 Sub-Items & Features

### 1. Asset Inventory

A comprehensive list of all company assets with details including asset type, name, serial number, condition, and current assignment status. Supports filtering by type, status, and assigned employee.

**Steps to use Asset Inventory:**
1. Navigate to the Asset Management module from the sidebar or module home page
2. Click on "Asset Inventory" from the sub-menu
3. Use the available options to create, view, edit, or manage records
4. Apply filters or search as needed to find specific items
5. Save or submit your changes when complete

### 2. Assign Assets

Assign assets to employees with date tracking and condition notes. The system records the assignment date and maintains history of all asset assignments and returns.

**Steps to use Assign Assets:**
1. Navigate to the Asset Management module from the sidebar or module home page
2. Click on "Assign Assets" from the sub-menu
3. Use the available options to create, view, edit, or manage records
4. Apply filters or search as needed to find specific items
5. Save or submit your changes when complete

### 3. Requests

Employees can submit asset requests which are then reviewed and approved by administrators. The request workflow includes justification, priority, and approval chain.

**Steps to use Requests:**

## 💡 Tips & Best Practices

1. Record serial numbers and asset tags when adding new assets for easy tracking.
2. Conduct regular asset audits to verify physical inventory matches system records.
3. Use the asset condition field to track wear and plan for replacements proactively.
4. Ensure all assets are returned and accounted for during employee exit processing.
5. Categorize assets by type for easier management and reporting.
