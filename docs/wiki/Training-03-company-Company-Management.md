## 📋 Module Overview

The Company Management module allows administrators to configure and manage company-wide settings, branch offices, and organizational policies. This module is essential for multi-tenant setups where each company has its own isolated data environment. Administrators can update company information, manage branch locations, and define HR policies that apply across the organization. The module also handles company registration details such as GST and PAN numbers.

The Company Management module is accessible from both the sidebar navigation and the module home page grid. It contains the following sub-items that provide specialized functionality within the module.

---

## 🔧 Sub-Items & Features

### 1. Company Info

View and edit company details including name, code, industry, address, contact information, GST number, PAN number, and subscription plan. Company info is used across all modules for branding and compliance.

**Steps to use Company Info:**
1. Navigate to the Company Management module from the sidebar or module home page
2. Click on "Company Info" from the sub-menu
3. Use the available options to create, view, edit, or manage records
4. Apply filters or search as needed to find specific items
5. Save or submit your changes when complete

### 2. Branches

Manage company branch offices with location details, office capacity, and assigned departments. Branch information is used for attendance location tracking and asset assignment.

Create, edit, and manage company HR policies including leave policies, attendance rules, reimbursement guidelines, and code of conduct. Policies can be categorized and versioned for compliance tracking.

## 💡 Tips & Best Practices

1. Set up company information completely before adding employees to ensure proper data association.
2. Use the company code system for multi-tenant isolation — each company has a unique code.
3. Keep GST and PAN numbers accurate for payroll and compliance purposes.
4. Define clear policies and make them accessible to all employees for transparency.
5. Regularly review and update company policies to stay compliant with labor laws.
