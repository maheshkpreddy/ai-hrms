## 📋 Role Overview

The L&D Manager role manages learning, development, and performance programs across the organization. L&D Managers have access to the Learning & Development module for course management and enrollment tracking, the Performance module for review oversight, and self-service for personal needs. They can create courses, track learning progress, manage certifications, and oversee performance review cycles. This role is designed for training managers and L&D professionals who drive skill development initiatives.

## 🔄 Key Workflows

The following are the primary workflows you will perform as a **L&D Manager**:

1. Creating and managing training courses and learning paths
2. Enrolling employees in courses and tracking completion
3. Managing certification programs and tracking expiry dates
4. Overseeing performance review cycles and providing guidance
5. Analyzing skill gaps and recommending targeted training
6. Generating learning and development reports for leadership
