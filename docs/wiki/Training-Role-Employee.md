## Role Overview

This training covers the features and workflows available for the **Employee** role.

## Key Topics Covered

### 1. Employee: Self-Service Portal

- Check in and check out for daily attendance
- Apply for leaves and view leave balance
- Download payslips and tax documents
- Update personal profile and documents
- Chat with AI HR Assistant for instant help

**Narration**: As an Employee, your primary interface is the Self-Service Portal. Check in and out for attendance, apply for leaves, download payslips, update your profile, and chat with the AI HR Assistant for instant help.

### 2. Employee: Getting Started

- Log in with your company code, email, and password
- Your dashboard shows attendance status and pending items
- Use the sidebar to navigate between your modules
- Check in when you arrive at the office
- Apply for leave through the Self-Service module

**Narration**: Getting started is simple. Log in with your company code, email, and password. Your dashboard shows attendance and pending items. Use the sidebar to navigate. Check in when you arrive, and apply for leave through Self-Service when needed.
