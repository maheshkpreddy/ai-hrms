## 📋 Role Overview

The Department Manager role focuses on team management, performance oversight, and attendance tracking for a specific department. Managers can view their team members, approve leaves, conduct performance reviews, and track learning progress. They have visibility into employee details and attendance data but limited access to payroll and system configuration. This role is designed for team leads and department heads who manage people and need insight into team performance.

## 🔄 Key Workflows

The following are the primary workflows you will perform as a **Department Manager**:

1. Monitoring team attendance and approving leave requests
2. Conducting performance reviews and setting goals for team members
3. Tracking team learning and certification progress
4. Viewing employee details within their department
5. Managing daily team operations through the dashboard
6. Recommending training and development opportunities for team members
