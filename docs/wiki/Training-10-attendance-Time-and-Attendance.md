## 📋 Module Overview

The Time & Attendance module provides comprehensive workforce time tracking capabilities. Employees can mark attendance with check-in and check-out times, and the system automatically calculates work hours, overtime, and late arrivals. The module supports multiple shift configurations, holiday calendars, and bulk attendance processing. Detailed reports and export capabilities enable HR teams to analyze attendance patterns and ensure payroll accuracy.

The Time & Attendance module is accessible from both the sidebar navigation and the module home page grid. It contains the following sub-items that provide specialized functionality within the module.

---

## 🔧 Sub-Items & Features

### 1. Mark Attendance

Record daily attendance with check-in and check-out times. Supports location-based attendance marking, automatic late detection based on shift schedule, and half-day tracking. The system calculates total work hours automatically.

**Steps to use Mark Attendance:**
1. Navigate to the Time & Attendance module from the sidebar or module home page
2. Click on "Mark Attendance" from the sub-menu

## 💡 Tips & Best Practices

1. Configure shifts with appropriate grace periods to accommodate minor delays without flagging late arrivals.
2. Set up the holiday calendar at the beginning of each year for proper leave planning.
3. Use bulk attendance marking for days when the system is unavailable or for retroactive entries.
4. Export monthly attendance reports for payroll processing to ensure accurate salary calculations.
5. Encourage employees to mark attendance at the actual check-in time for accurate tracking.
