## How to Access Training Videos

1. **In-App**: Navigate to **Help & Training** → **Training Videos** tab in the sidebar
2. **GitHub Wiki**: Browse individual module training guides from the sidebar
3. **Interactive Tutorials**: Click any video card in the Knowledge Hub to start a step-by-step walkthrough

## Video Tutorial Player

The in-app tutorial player provides an interactive step-by-step experience:
- Click **Play** to start the walkthrough
- Navigate between steps using **Next** and **Previous** buttons
- Click any step number to jump directly to that step
- Completed steps are marked with a green checkmark
- Close the player at any time to return to the video list
