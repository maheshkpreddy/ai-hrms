## 📋 Role Overview

The Payroll Specialist role is focused on payroll processing and financial operations. Payroll Specialists have access to the payroll module for salary processing, tax calculations, and report generation, along with basic dashboard and self-service access. They can view HR data for payroll purposes but cannot modify employee records or access other HR modules. This role is designed for finance team members who handle monthly payroll runs, expense approvals, and financial compliance.

## 🔄 Key Workflows

The following are the primary workflows you will perform as a **Payroll Specialist**:

1. Processing monthly payroll for all employees
2. Reviewing and approving employee expense claims
3. Generating payroll reports for compliance and audits
4. Managing tax deductions and statutory compliance
5. Viewing attendance data for payroll accuracy
6. Handling salary revisions and arrears processing
