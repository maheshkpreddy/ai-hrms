## 📋 Module Overview

The Payroll & Expenses module handles the complete payroll lifecycle from processing monthly salaries to managing employee expense claims. It automates salary calculations including basic pay, HRA, DA, conveyance, medical allowance, and bonuses, while also computing deductions for PF, ESI, tax, and professional tax. The expense management system handles employee expense submissions, approval workflows, and reimbursement tracking with detailed audit trails.

The Payroll & Expenses module is accessible from both the sidebar navigation and the module home page grid. It contains the following sub-items that provide specialized functionality within the module.

---

## 🔧 Sub-Items & Features

### 1. Process Payroll

Run monthly payroll processing with automated calculation of earnings (basic, HRA, DA, conveyance, medical, bonus) and deductions (PF, ESI, tax, professional tax). Supports payroll review, approval, and payment status tracking (pending, processed, paid).

**Steps to use Process Payroll:**
1. Navigate to the Payroll & Expenses module from the sidebar or module home page
2. Click on "Process Payroll" from the sub-menu
3. Use the available options to create, view, edit, or manage records
4. Apply filters or search as needed to find specific items
5. Save or submit your changes when complete

### 2. Expenses

Submit, approve, and track employee expense claims across categories (travel, accommodation, equipment, food, etc.). Includes approval workflow, receipt attachment, and reimbursement status tracking.

**Steps to use Expenses:**
1. Navigate to the Payroll & Expenses module from the sidebar or module home page
2. Click on "Expenses" from the sub-menu
3. Use the available options to create, view, edit, or manage records
4. Apply filters or search as needed to find specific items
5. Save or submit your changes when complete

### 3. Reports

Generate payroll reports including salary registers, tax reports, PF/ESI reports, and expense summaries. Reports support department-wise, employee-wise, and period-wise filtering with export capabilities.

## 💡 Tips & Best Practices

1. Always verify attendance data before running payroll to ensure accurate calculations.
2. Review payroll in "processed" status before marking as "paid" to catch any errors.
3. Set up expense approval workflows with multiple levels for high-value claims.
4. Generate monthly payroll reports for compliance and audit requirements.
5. Keep tax declaration data updated to ensure accurate TDS calculations.
