## 📋 Module Overview

The Meeting Management module streamlines the scheduling, tracking, and documentation of meetings across the organization. Users can schedule meetings with participants, set agendas, and send invitations. The module tracks upcoming and past meetings, manages participant RSVPs, and maintains meeting notes and action items. Integration with calendar systems ensures no scheduling conflicts and improves meeting productivity.

The Meeting Management module is accessible from both the sidebar navigation and the module home page grid. It contains the following sub-items that provide specialized functionality within the module.

---

## 🔧 Sub-Items & Features

### 1. Upcoming Meetings

View all scheduled meetings with details including title, date, time, location, participants, and agenda. Supports filtering by date range and participant. Includes RSVP tracking.

**Steps to use Upcoming Meetings:**
1. Navigate to the Meeting Management module from the sidebar or module home page
2. Click on "Upcoming Meetings" from the sub-menu
3. Use the available options to create, view, edit, or manage records
4. Apply filters or search as needed to find specific items
5. Save or submit your changes when complete

### 2. Schedule Meeting

Create new meetings with title, date, time, location (physical or virtual), participant selection, agenda items, and meeting notes template. Sends automatic invitations to participants.

**Steps to use Schedule Meeting:**
1. Navigate to the Meeting Management module from the sidebar or module home page
2. Click on "Schedule Meeting" from the sub-menu
3. Use the available options to create, view, edit, or manage records
4. Apply filters or search as needed to find specific items
5. Save or submit your changes when complete

### 3. Past Meetings

Access history of completed meetings with notes, action items, and participant feedback. Supports searching and filtering past meetings for reference and follow-up tracking.

**Steps to use Past Meetings:**
1. Navigate to the Meeting Management module from the sidebar or module home page
2. Click on "Past Meetings" from the sub-menu
3. Use the available options to create, view, edit, or manage records
4. Apply filters or search as needed to find specific items
5. Save or submit your changes when complete

## 💡 Tips & Best Practices

1. Always include an agenda when scheduling meetings to keep discussions focused.
2. Set meeting durations appropriately — shorter meetings tend to be more productive.
3. Document action items and assignees during meetings for accountability.
4. Review past meeting notes before scheduling follow-ups to ensure continuity.
5. Use the virtual meeting option for remote participants to improve attendance.
