## Video 1: Getting Started with eh2r AI Dashboard (8:24)

### [0:30 - 1:30] Logging In

**Voiceover**: "The main dashboard gives you an at-a-glance view of your organization's key HR metrics. ### [3:00 - 4:30] Quick Actions
**Voiceover**: "The Quick Actions section provides one-click access to common tasks — add an employee, process payroll, post a job, run reports, schedule an interview, or approve leaves. Simply click any action card to navigate directly to that module."

### [4:30 - 6:00] Sidebar Navigation
**Voiceover**: "The left sidebar contains all 27 modules organized by function. Click any module to expand its sub-items. Modules marked with an 'AI' badge are powered by artificial intelligence for enhanced capabilities. Use the search bar at the top of the sidebar to quickly find any module."

### [6:00 - 7:30] Notifications & Profile
**Voiceover**: "The notification bell in the top header shows pending approvals and system alerts. Your profile menu in the top-right provides access to your profile settings and sign-out option. The sidebar also shows your profile at the bottom with a quick sign-out button."

**Screen**: Click notification bell, show dropdown. Click profile avatar, show dropdown menu.

### [7:30 - 8:24] Wrap Up
**Voiceover**: "That's your quick start guide to the eh2r AI dashboard. In the next videos, we'll dive deeper into each module. Check the Help & Training section for more tutorials and the GitHub Wiki for detailed documentation."

## Video 8: Daily Attendance Tracking & Check-In (6:18)

### [0:00 - 0:30] Introduction
**Voiceover**: "This tutorial shows how employees check in and out, and how managers track attendance across their teams."

**Screen**: Show the Self-Service or Attendance module.

### [0:30 - 2:00] Employee Check-In
**Voiceover**: "Employees can check in from the Self-Service module by clicking the 'Check In' button, or from the Time & Attendance module. The system records the check-in time, and optionally the location and source (web, mobile, biometric)."

**Screen**: Navigate to Self-Service, click Check In button, show the confirmation.

### [2:00 - 3:30] Check-Out & Work Hours
**Voiceover**: "At the end of the workday, click 'Check Out'. The system automatically calculates the total work hours based on check-in and check-out times. If you forget to check out, your manager can regularize the attendance."

**Screen**: Click Check Out, show the calculated work hours.

### [3:30 - 5:00] Attendance History
**Voiceover**: "View your attendance history from the Attendance module. You can see daily records with check-in/out times, work hours, and status (present, late, absent, half-day). Use filters to view specific date ranges."

**Screen**: Show attendance history table, apply date filters.

### [5:00 - 6:18] Manager View
**Voiceover**: "Managers can view their team's attendance from the Attendance module. Filter by department, status, or date. Regularize attendance records for team members who missed check-ins."

**Screen**: Switch to manager view, show team attendance, demonstrate regularization.
